Homemade Alfredo pasta

Description

This is a recipe that my mom named "Love Pasta" because she always made it for a special occasion. It's a very tasty and creamy pasta, and it's pretty easy to make.

It's also a little personal variaton to the original recipe, and that's why she decided to give it a different name as a kind of familiar thing.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Spinach
  • Mushrooms
  • Cooking cream
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Chili flakes
  • Oregano
  • Parsley
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Heat a pan and add butter
  3. Add garlic and onion
  4. When the onion is cooked, add chicken and cook it until it's brown
    • Season the chicken with salt, black pepper and paprika
  5. Add spinach and mushrooms and cook them
  6. Add cooking cream and parmesan cheese
  7. Add salt, black pepper and oregano
  8. When the sauce is ready, add the pasta and mix it
  9. Serve and add parmesan cheese, chili flakes and parsley to garnish
  10. Invite me over to try it
