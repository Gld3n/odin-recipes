Patatas bravas

Description

Patatas bravas are a typical Spanish bar dish consisting of large diced potatoes fried in olive oil and served with salsa brava, a spicy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tabasco sauce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes
  2. Heat a pan with olive oil
  3. Add the potatoes and fry them until they're cooked
  4. Remove the potatoes from the pan and put them on a plate with paper towels to absorb the excess oil
  5. Heat a pan with olive oil
  6. Add garlic and onion
  7. When the onion is cooked, add tomato sauce, tabasco sauce, salt, black pepper and paprika
  8. When the sauce is ready, add the potatoes and mix them
  9. Serve and add mayonnaise and parsley to garnish
  10. Give me some for my mom to try it
