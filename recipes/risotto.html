Chicken & mushrooms risotto

Description

The chicken and mushroom risotto is a typical Italian dish that consists of rice cooked in a broth to a creamy consistency.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Mushrooms
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Arborio rice
  • Chicken broth
  • White wine
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Rosemary

Steps

  1. Heat a pan with olive oil
  2. Add garlic and onion
  3. When the onion is cooked, add chicken and cook it until it's brown
    • Season the chicken with salt, black pepper and paprika
  4. Add mushrooms and cook them
  5. Add arborio rice and cook it for a few minutes
  6. Add white wine and cook it until it's absorbed
  7. Add chicken broth and cook it until it's absorbed
  8. Repeat the previous step until the rice is cooked
  9. When the rice is cooked, add butter and parmesan cheese
  10. Serve and add parmesan cheese, rosemary and black pepper to garnish
